About us

Author：M2SG Official         Date：14/8/2022

Who We Are?

We are the M2SG Organizing Committee. M2SG, or Minecraft Small Server Games, is a conference for all Small servers, and we are committed to making it easier for the vast majority of Small Server players to participate in competitive events. Also committed to let small servers for hosting M2SG and can get better development.

Who makes up the M2SG Organizing Committee?

The M2SG Organizing Committee is currently composed of:
  • EchoicManter (Chairman of the Organizing Committee)
  • soonwinterSB (Head of Logistics Team)
  • genius (Redstone Consultants)
